Prepaid Telecom Billing System

A prepaid mobile operator's Business Support System covering customers, SIMs, product catalog, wallets, recharge distribution, and real-time charging driven by Diameter credit-control messages.

Business Overview

A prepaid mobile operator's Business Support System (BSS) — the business and data layer behind the network. Customers load money into a Wallet, buy a Tariff Plan that credits Balances (data MB, voice minutes, SMS count), then consume services. Every chargeable event (data session, call, SMS) arrives as a Diameter CCR message, is rated and debited from the appropriate balance in real time, and answered with a CCA carrying granted quota or a final-unit indication.

Scope is prepaid only: pay-before-use with real-time enforcement. No postpaid invoicing, dunning, or credit scoring. Retail distribution (recharge vouchers, dealer commissions) and self-care channels (USSD, app, IVR, POS) are first-class. Diameter is simulated as JSON events rather than running over the wire.

Reports & Analytics 10

  • ARPU & Revenue Mix flat — Average Revenue Per User, revenue split by plan / PAYG / overage per period.
  • Usage Dashboard (MOU, Data MB, SMS) flat — Per-subscriber and per-segment consumption across rating groups.
  • Recharge Trends hierarchical — Volume and value by channel, denomination, partner, and region.
  • Voucher Lifecycle flat — Generated → distributed → sold → redeemed rates, expiry leakage.
  • Plan Uptake & Renewal hierarchical — Active assignments, auto-renew success, allowance exhaustion distribution.
  • Charging Success Rate flat — % CCRs returning 2001 vs. 4xxx/5xxx, FUI trigger rate per RG.
  • Customer Health & Churn hierarchical — Active subs, churn rate, port-out rate, net adds, segment cuts.
  • Partner Performance flat — Recharge volume, commission accrued, settlement aging per partner.
  • Notification Delivery flat — Send / delivered / bounce rate per channel and template.
  • Case Backlog hierarchical — Open / aging cases by category, priority, assignee, and resolution SLA.

Business Process Flow

  1. Customer Acquisition
    • Lead / walk-in via retail partner, app, or online portal.
    • KYC capture — document(s) uploaded to Customer Identifications.
    • MSISDN selection from MSISDN Pool (reserve → assign).
    • SIM allocation from SIM Inventory (allocated → activated).
    • Customer + Subscription + Wallet created; welcome SMS dispatched.
  2. Recharge / Top-up
    • Customer initiates via voucher, USSD, app, retail POS, IVR, or online.
    • Voucher PIN validated or gateway payment authorized.
    • Wallet Transaction posted (credit); Partner Commission accrued at 3%.
    • Notification Sent: RECHARGE_OK.
  3. Plan Purchase
    • Customer selects Product Offering via self-care channel.
    • Wallet debited; Subscription Plan Assignment created.
    • Balance rows created (one per Plan Allowance × RG); Notification Sent: PLAN_ACTIVATED.
  4. Real-time Charging (the Diameter loop)
    • CCR-I — identify Subscription → check Balance → reserve chunk → CCA 2001.
    • CCR-U — record used quota → decrement Balance → grant next chunk or FUI at zero.
    • CCR-T — finalize used amount → refund unused reserve → close Charging Session → generate CDR.
    • CCR-E — single-shot debit for SMS / one-off purchase.
    • Dedup key: (SessionID, RequestNumber); ordering by timestamp.
  5. Balance Enforcement & FUI
    • overage_action = block → CCA 4012, fui_action = TERMINATE.
    • overage_action = charge_from_wallet → rate from Wallet at Rate Card until empty.
    • Low-balance notifications at 80% / 95% / 100% consumed.
  6. Plan Expiry
    • Allowance depletion or validity expiry (whichever first).
    • Subscription Plan Assignment flipped to expired; auto-renewal attempted if wallet ≥ price.
    • Fallback to PAYG or base plan if stacked.
  7. Balance Transfer (P2P gifting)
    • Validate limits and KYC → debit source wallet → credit destination → log Balance Transfer.
  8. Churn / Port-Out
    • Inactivity suspension after N days → termination after grace → MSISDN quarantined 90 days.
    • Port-out: status flipped, final CDR settled with new operator.
  9. Partner Settlement
    • Periodic aggregation of accrued Partner Commissions → payment → status → settled.
  10. Automation
    • 3 workflows: Plan Purchase, Recharge Processing, New Activation.
    • Scheduled: low-balance alerts (15 min), plan expiry (daily), partner settlement (weekly), fraud velocity (15 min).
    • All aggregation via ERPAI rollup / formula / lookup — workflows only for cross-table record creation.
