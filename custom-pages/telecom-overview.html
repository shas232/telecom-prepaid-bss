Business Overview

A prepaid telecom Business Support System (BSS) delivering end-to-end operations for a mobile operator: customer & subscription lifecycle, SIM and MSISDN management, tariff-plan catalog with bundles and booster stacking, a monetary wallet alongside multi-balance service buckets (data / voice / SMS / bonus), real-time Diameter Gy/Ro online charging with priority-based balance depletion, CDR generation, partner & channel distribution with commissioning, and a full customer-service surface (cases, interactions, notifications).

The model captures every artefact touched during the prepaid charging flow — CCR-I initiations, incremental CCR-U reauthorizations, CCR-T terminations, and exception CCR-E events — flowing through Charging Sessions → Usage Transactions → Call Detail Records, with rollup totals on subscriptions and balance buckets.

Business Process Flow

  1. Customer Onboarding
    • Agent or self-care captures identity; record created in Customers with Customer Identifications for KYC.
    • MSISDN pulled from MSISDN Pool; SIM allocated from SIM Inventory; Subscriptions row created and activated.
    • Customer Lifecycle Events log records the activation; welcome SMS sent via Notifications Sent.
  2. Plan Selection & Provisioning
    • Customer picks a Tariff Plan; an Order with Order Items is created.
    • Wallet is debited via Wallet Transactions; service buckets are seeded in Balances (data / voice / SMS / bonus) with priority-on-charge.
    • Optional Bundles / boosters stack additional buckets on top of the base plan.
  3. Recharge Flow
    • Voucher from Recharge Vouchers, or partner POS via Distribution Partners, creates a Recharges row.
    • Wallet credited (Wallet Transactions); commission row inserted in Partner Commissions according to Partner Contracts.
  4. Real-time Charging (Gy)
    • Network element (PGW / MSC / SMSC) opens a Charging Sessions row with CCR-I.
    • Per event, a Usage Transactions row records Requested / Granted / Used Service-Unit, with the rating-group determined by Services.
    • Rating engine applies Business Rules and depletes Balances in priority order; bonus buckets deplete before paid ones.
    • Session ends on CCR-T; Call Detail Records settlement row is written.
  5. Promotions & Loyalty
    • Eligibility engine checks Promotions; on hit a Promotion Redemptions row and a Bonus Grants balance entry are created.
    • Friends and Family Groups / Closed User Groups redirect rating to discounted tariffs for on-net on-group calls.
  6. Customer Care
    • All inbound touchpoints land in Customer Interactions; escalations create Cases with SLA + status.
    • CSR can adjust Balances, issue Bonus Grants, or trigger Balance Transfers between subscribers.
  7. Expiry & Churn
    • Daily sweep suspends Subscriptions whose validity has expired; status transition logged in Subscription Status History.
    • Low-balance / expiring-plan notifications trigger retention promos.
  8. Partner & Channel Ops
    • Channels (retail, USSD, self-care, B2B) route orders and recharges to the right partner.
    • Partner Commissions ledger is settled on cadence defined in Partner Contracts.
  9. Automation
    • Workflows: welcome notification on activation, low-balance alert, plan-expiry reminder, plan-purchase confirmation, daily CDR summary.
    • Rollups refresh in near-realtime: Used / Remaining Amount per balance, Lifetime Recharge per customer, Active Subscribers per plan.
