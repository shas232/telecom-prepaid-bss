Regulatory Notice — MNP shelved in Botswana (BOCRA, April 2021)
Mobile Number Portability is currently out of regulatory scope in Botswana — BOCRA shelved the 2013 directive following a 2021 market-readiness review. The MNP tables below are retained in the schema for future re-enablement. Cancelled records are reference scenarios. Vanity number auctions and recycling rules remain fully active.
